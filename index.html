About us

For 5 years now we have been providing aid to those in needy situations in Afghanistan such as widows, orphans, and those with disabilities.

With your donations, we managed to help 20 families in 2016, 27 families in 2017, 42 families in 2018, and 39 families in 2019.

2020

We are raising donations for the 5th consecutive year and, once again, ask for your financial support.

The current COVID-19 pandemic has brought severe strain to vulnerable people. In Afghanistan, there are many who rely on their daily earnings to purchase the food they will eat that night. They lack safety nets, and staying in quarantine means that they will not be able to bring food to the table.

This Ramadan, I encourage you to help me expand the cause so that we can help more families in desperate need of food.
